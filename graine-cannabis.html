About Cannabis

Cannabis () is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cannabaceae. The number of species within the genus is contested. Three species might be acknowledged: Cannabis sativa, C. indica, and C. ruderalis. Alternatively, C. ruderalis might be included within C. sativa, or all 3 might be treated as subspecies of C. sativa, or C. sativa may be approved as a single concentrated species. The genus is commonly approved as being indigenous to and originating from Asia. The plant is likewise referred to as hemp, although this term is commonly used to refer only to varieties of Marijuana cultivated for non-drug usage. Marijuana has actually long been used for hemp fiber, hemp seeds and their oils, hemp leaves for use as veggies and as juice. Industrial hemp items are made from marijuana plants chosen to create an abundance of fibre. Marijuana also has a lengthy background of being used for medicinal functions, and as an entertainment medication known as marijuana or weed. Various cannabis pressures have actually been bred, commonly selectively to produce high or low levels of tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), a cannabinoid and the plant's major psychedelic component. Compounds such as hashish and hash oil are extracted from the plant.
